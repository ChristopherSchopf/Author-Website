Books I've Written

Abandoned Kingdom

Uncover the haunting secrets of a once-great civilization, revealing the mysteries hidden within crumbling palaces and ancient artifacts.

This gripping exploration invites readers to question the fragility of human achievement and the moral dilemmas of exposing long-buried truths.


Turkish Origins

Turkish Origins traces the journey of the Turkic peoples from Central Asia to Anatolia, where their traditions and languages took root.

The book explores how these influences shaped the rich cultural heritage of modern Turkey.


Sword of Legends

Sword of Legends follows the journey of an ancient blade, passed down through generations, at the heart of epic battles and forgotten kingdoms.

This thrilling tale unravels the sword's mysterious origins and its pivotal role in shaping history.
